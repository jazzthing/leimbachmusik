My biography in a small nutshell

The beginning
I was born and raised in Ruda Śląska in Poland. When I was about 13 years old I've started music education in a local music school where I've been studying classical music and percussion instruments like xylophone, snare drum, vibraphone, marimba and timpani drums. In 2004 I've graduated from Music High School in Katowice.

Jazz in Krakow
After high school I’ve moved to Cracow to keep studying classical music at Cracow Academy of Music and quite quickly got myself immersed in cracow jazz scene spending hours in local jazz clubs where I could learn directly about jazz music from some cracow greatest jazz musicians (Janusz Muniak, Wojciech Groborz to name some). I’ve also had a chance to play with Bigband Małopolski and Big Band of Cracow Academy of Music.

Between Cracow and Paris...
In summer 2007 I’ve met some musicians from Paris. Curious to discover new horizons, I’ve made a brake in my studies and moved to Paris for about 8 months, where I've been spending my time roaming in parisian jazz clubs, jam sessions and festivals. I've decided to go back to Poland to finish my master in classical percussion, which I made in june 2010. I’ve also started a group "4Aheads Agata Nowak Quartet" (*). We've been playing my new compositions written in Paris in 2009 and later on.

Jazz in Paris
In september 2010 I have started studying in Centre of Music of Didier Lockwood in Dammarie les Lys in France (about 50 km from Paris) which lasted about a year. I’ve managed to reconstruct my quartet there and record a few of my compositions in studio.

Pedagogical Work
In 2011 I moved closer to Paris and started comprehensive work as a classical percussion and drumset teacher in Paris-Vellée de la Marne Conservatory and Conservatory of Music in Senlis, receiving about 40 individual students in a week, organising percussion ensembles and student concerts, and coordinating percussion department in Paris-Vellée de la Marne Conservatory.

A musician
However I’ve never stopped to play and compose music. I’ve been active on Parisian jazz scene playing occasionally in clubs. I’ve cooperated regularly with a world/oriental jazz group "Mizmar" with whom in 2014 we’ve recorded an album "Mix'it".

Life in Zürich
In february 2016, following my husband, I've moved to Zürich in Switzerland. Here, I continue my musical adventures with different musicians, bands, orchestras and also as a music teacher.
(*) Nowak is my maiden name
