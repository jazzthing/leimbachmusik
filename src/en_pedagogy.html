Welcome! My name is Agata Husser.
I am a graduate in music pedagogy and master in music performance (percussion instruments).
I teach the following instruments:

  • drum set : all styles, from beginner to advanced level;
  • piano and e-piano: all styles, from beginner to intermediate level;
  • xylophone and vibraphone: all styles, from beginner to advanced level.

Lessons are adapted to the taste of music and the individual student's pace of progress. Learning by ear and developing a musical intuition is privileged, all courses contain elements of musical theory necessary to acquire knowledge of musical language.

Teaching is available in English, Polish, German or French language.

I’ve been teaching music for over 10 years, working with children and teenagers as well with adults. I’ve been teaching individual students but I've been also working with groups of students in primary schools, public and private music schools.
Since 2011 to 2016 I’ve been working as a drum set and classical percussion teacher in three public music schools in France receiving around 40 individual students in a week, organising percussion ensembles and student's concerts, and coordinating department of percussion instruments.
